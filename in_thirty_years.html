Care Trade

Date: 2021

Brief: Create a social media layout for Care Trade.

Outcome:
CareTrade is a charity that supports and prepares autistic people for employment. It was founded to address the lack of opportunities for autistic adults leaving school and beyond to lead purposeful and fulfilled lives.


Their flagship programme is The Autism Project (TAP). TAP is a two year supported internship programme for autistic 18-25 year olds who want to prepare for employment. They also provide ‘in work support’ for autistic employees (funded by Access to Work or the employer).


My main responsibility throughout this project was to create a social media strategy. I have created a simple and clean layout with strong messages they want to spread about autistic people.
