Grenfell x NIKE

Date: 2023

Brief: A project conducted by the studio Brothers & Sisters, one of the most popular design studios in the UK. The project consisted in producing a design for a t-shirt for the football club Grenfell. This brief is also sponsored by Nike which aims to accomplish children’s dreams through sports. The project “Grenfell x Nike Fabric of the Community” is a step further to connecting dreams and sports.

Outcome:
I was in charge of coming up with the kit design for the football shirt. That includes an idea concept, illustration for the printing of the outcome and marketing to sponsor and rise sales. The main idea for the kit was titled "Embracing Our Land!" which was meant to bring everyone together as community to look forward for a better future.
