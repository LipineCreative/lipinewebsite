Who is Lipine?


Lipine is a Portuguese Graphic Designer/ Illustrator based in London. She graduated in 2023 with a Bachelor's in Graphic & Media Design at UAL: University Arts of London.


Through her journey as an artist, Lipine has worked with clients all over the world which allowed her to meet amazing creative people. Her most recognised work was the award certificates produced for the biggest event of the Nordics - SDG Tech Awards 2021 Denmark.


Right now she has been working on a book soon to be published in Portugal and Brazil. Hand-drawn illustrations and story have been both produced by Lipine. The book will be published under the Portuguese publisher "Novo Capítulo". You can find more information about it by accessing the "Portfolio" section.


Lipine also published a portfolio with some of her works as an artist to celebrate her brand.
