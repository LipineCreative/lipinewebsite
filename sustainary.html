Sustainary

Date: 2021 - 2022

Brief: Create the assets for the biggest Nordic Sustainable Event - SDG Tech Awards Denmark 2021. As well as social media posts to promote the client's event.

Outcome:
I have been responsible to create the digital and print assets for Sustainary.


Posts to update their social media are highly demanded. There is an everyday challenge to make different posts while recycling their brand identity to make it different. Printed posters and flyers are requested monthly for various events.


The biggest project was the creation of the award certificates handled to the winners of companies awarded for their sustainable work and products. In total were made 13 certificates with 13 different categories. A booklet was also made as part of the event to be handled physically and available online for company event information purposes.
